Youth-led, scout-informed, and publicly crowdsourced. AstroScouts is a park-rating ecosystem by the people who walk the trails. not just the ones who pave them.

Why This matters

Parks are more than just green spaces—they are community sanctuaries, places of healing, and
reflections of how we care
for each other and the planet.

But not every park is equally accessible, clean, or sustainable.

That’s why we’re building a platform powered by Scouts and public voices to evaluate and elevate the places we love.

How It Works

1. Visit a Park

3. Help the Community

Your review helps others explore—and helps parks improve.
