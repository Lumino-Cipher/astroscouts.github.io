Our Mission

AstroScouts was born from the footsteps of youth—an effort to bring parks to light through accessibility, sustainability, and shared voices.

Parks are fading away.

24%
of teenagers report visiting a park more than once a month
86%
of public parks still lack ADA-compliant pathways

Parks have been warming twice as fast as the U.S. as a whole.

Many parks are frontline spaces for the climate impact, but lack the data to adapt.

Our Commitment to a Brighter Future for Parks.

Faced with the urgent challenges of declining engagement, accessibility gaps, and the profound impacts of climate change, AstroScouts is dedicated to empowering communities and preserving our natural spaces. We believe that by fostering a deeper connection with nature and providing transparent, actionable insights, we can build a more resilient and inclusive future for parks. Our approach is rooted in three core pillars:

Accessibility

Everyone deserves to experience nature—regardless of ability.

Sustainability

We measure impact as much as we admire beauty.

Environmental Honesty

Our ratings are sourced from those who walk the trails.

Our Journey So Far

September 19th, 2023

AstroScouts project approved by the Eagle Scout Board of Review, marking the official beginning of our journey.

June 23rd - July 28th, 2025

Intensive planning and coding phase, bringing the AstroScouts website to life from concept to digital reality.

July 11th, 2025

Website officially launched and discoverable by major search engines, opening our platform to the world.

July 16th, 2025

The first park review is submitted, a momentous step in building our community-driven park accessibility database.

October 5th, 2025

Partnership established with National Parks Conservancy, expanding our reach and impact.

January 1st, 2026

Launch of the AstroScouts mobile application, making park information accessible on the go.

June 15th, 2026

AstroScouts hosts its first nationwide "Accessible Trails" event, promoting inclusivity in nature.

December 31st, 2027

Achieve 10,000 unique park reviews, establishing AstroScouts as a leading resource for park accessibility data.

July 20th, 2030

Integration of real-time climate impact data for all listed parks, empowering visitors and park management.
